Master Chens Traditonal Chinese Dishes. So traditional you may speak Mandarin after eating here!

It's no secret, to truly enjoy and appreciate a culture, a person has to be totally immersed in it. At Master Chens, you wont have a different option! Upon entering, our lovely hostess will ask questions in a language you can't speak. Don't be nervous, we will sit you in a tabel that fits one less than your party has!

Once Seated. Our menu opens to the vast world of Traditional Chinese Dishes.

Our menu is huge..Or so it seems. Really, there's only about 6-7 different dishes, we just like to split them up into categories based on protein. MooShoo Pork, MooShoo Chicken, MooShoo Beef...All MooShoo, but totally different. Also, our menu includes wonderful, accurate translations of Chinese Script provided by Google; Chicken Rice Water Juice anyone!!!

And Finally, You are ready to leave. Your server will place a bill on your table, it's time to leave.

Once your server has put your bill on the table, it means we have decided you have had a good expierence and you are ready to tell your friends! Don't try to split your bill between your table, you wont be able to read it! In all honesty, we take a guess on how much you owe us once you get to the register.
